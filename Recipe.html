How to cook ugali in Kenya

Did you knao that I am updating this from the Kali Environment!!

Ugali is the most common food in Kenya

I will teach you how to have it ready on the table for lunch or supper

Ingredients

  1. Water.
  2. Maize Flour.

Here is how to start.

  1. Boil water to 100 degres celcius.
  2. pour Flour and start stirring.
  3. Stir until it becomes medium to hard while adding more flour.
